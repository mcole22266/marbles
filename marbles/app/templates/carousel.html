Meet the Racers

Black Jack

Height: 14mm
Weight: 0.16oz
Born: Los Angelos, California - 1994

Black Jack is a California kid through and through. He made his fame early, at a young age, by posting highlight videos to his YouTube channel and generating quite a social media following. He calls his fans “Black Jackers” and they are some of the most dedicated fans out there. He made his mark on the Marble Race Scene by having one of the closest 1st place finishes in Marble Race History. He is assisted by his long time friend and on-field reporter, Josh Fuentes.

Blue Gooze

Height: 14mm
Weight: 0.16oz
Born: Irving, Texas - 1992

Blue Gooze grew up in Irving with everyone knowing he was going to be a star. A young Phenom mad his way to the big stage at a young age and has been up here ever since. Blue Gooze always keeps himself in the hunt with leading the league in top 3 finishes. He is best friends with his childhood best friend and on field reporter Trevor Story.

Green Goblin

Height: 14mm
Weight: 0.16oz
Born: London, United Kingdom - 1997

Green Goblin was born and raised in London, United Kingdom, and trained with some of the most elite trainers overseas. GG moved to the United States to Marble Race professionally and hit the “Track Running” by winning is first two ever professional races. He is the Youngest one of the Marble Stars but has put his ball in the faces of all of his competitors to prove he belongs. It’s a family affair with Green Goblin because his brother, Taylor Cook, is also his trainer and on-field reporter.

White Lightning

Height: 14mm
Weight: 0.16oz
Born: Tampa Bay, Florida - 1990

White Lightning has had an uphill battle to get back here in the limelight, he was once a young phenom who was cocky beyond belief. He walked and talked like he owned the world and nothing could stop him. A Rough patch hit him and he hit rock bottom, with all of his bridges burned… he had no one. No one except Parker Sheets, his on field reporter from day one. He trained harder than ever and is now back on top to prove he belongs.
